How to Train Your Dragon

A magical journey of friendship, bravery, and dragons.

🐉 Plot

In the Viking village of Berk, young Hiccup aspires to become a dragon slayer like his tribe, but ends up befriending one instead — the mysterious and powerful Night Fury, Toothless. Their bond changes everything the village believed about dragons.

ℹ️ Key Information

  • Release Year: 2010
  • Directors: Chris Sanders, Dean DeBlois
  • Production: DreamWorks Animation
  • Genre: Animation, Action, Adventure

🎭 Cast & Creative Team

  • Jay Baruchel as Hiccup
  • America Ferrera as Astrid
  • Gerard Butler as Stoick
  • Craig Ferguson as Gobber
  • Music by John Powell

👥 Characters

Toothless — the rare and intelligent Night Fury dragon.

Hiccup — a clever, curious Viking who changes the world.

Astrid — brave, bold, and always ready to fight for what's right.
